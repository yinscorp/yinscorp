Introducing Count

Convert your smartphone into interactive projector that responds to hand gestures!

Count is a high performance HD pico projector integrated with smartphone dock, an LED lamp, omnidirectional microphone and a stereo speaker that can all be connected via Bluetooth link. It also includes a USB port for smartphone charging and an HDMI port for connecting smartphone with the projector via MHL adapter or Apple's lightning-to-HDMI adapter. With all the above components controlled by the docked smartphone running our proprietary Yinscorp APP, Count is a versatile interactive projector that can project cloned or extended display of the phone, respond to your gestures on the projected screen, understand voice commands and create augmented reality platform

  • Smartphone Holder (Rotatable).

  • Portable Projector

  • 360-Degree Projection Rotation
